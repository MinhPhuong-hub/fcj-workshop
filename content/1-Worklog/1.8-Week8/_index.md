# Worklog Week 8: CloudWatch, logs, metrics, alarms, and evidence

**Time:** 01/06/2026 - 07/06/2026

## Week 8 objectives

- Understand CloudWatch Logs, Metrics, Alarms, and operational evidence.

- Prepare the audit/evidence plane for the final architecture.
